ABOUT US

Indian Policy Research Institute is a new versatile platform for Social Scientist to Showcase their talent & passion towards work.It is a fastest growing organization in the area of Education and Health.IPRI is a non-profitable organization founded by the group of students from different IIT's.We are working with the team of highly passionate,dedicated employees who work on their ethics and always ready to provide best services to our targeted customers with the best quality product.

OUR WORK ETHICS

OUR ETHICS
		Neither the markets nor aid alone can solve the problems of poverty. 
		More than two billion people around the world lack access to basic 
		goods and services—from clean water and electricity to an education
		and the freedom to participate in the economy. We’re here to change 
		that. Our vision is a world based on dignity, where every human being
		has the same opportunity. Rather than giving philanthropy away, 
		we invest it in companies and change makers.
